How to buy?

    1. Go to https://www.icoffee.com.ar
    2. Browse for available items or in-game currency.
    3. Once you click on a particular item or currency, we will automatically show you the best Offer from all the Sellers based on various factors like price and feedback. If quantity, price or delivery time does not suit you, always check other available Sellers below.
    4. Select Quantity that you would like to purchase.
    5. Click Checkout.
    6. On Summary page you will be asked to provide some extra information required for delivery like your character name. This depends on a particular game. Also, here you will be able to see payment summary:
      • Order Price.
      • Order Price after discount (if applicable).
      • Quantity and price per unit.
      • Now you have to pay via Paypal or other payment method.
    1. Click "Pay with Paypal" (you will see different buttons depending on payments methods).
    2. After completing the payment, you will be redirected to your Order.
    3. Once your payment is completed we contact you via email and give you all the seller in-game information.
    4. What is left is to meet with your seller in any major city (SW,IF,OG,UC) and complete the transaction.

In-game delivery

Buyer will not receive items or currency automatically. After purchase and when payment isaproved Buyer will be directed to Order page. There will be a live chat between Buyer and Seller to arrange the delivery. Buyer will need to log into game, wait for the Seller to send a trade request and accept it. Then Buyer should go back to Order page, click "Received item" and leave feedback.

Trade protection

Security Team
  • 8 days ago
  • Updated
Follow

In iCoffee Trade protection protects buyers against any attempted fraud. iCoffee takes full responsibility for the entire buying and selling process in the marketplace. Buyers are fully protected against sellers who do not deliver their items or deliver items that are different from the original listing.

iCoffee reserves the original payment until the item is marked as delivered and no complaints are raised. Once the item is delivered the amount paid is released to the seller. If a dispute arises, iCoffee protects the buyer by freezing the payment until the issue is resolved. Once the dispute is resolved, if the item was not delivered or was not as described in the listing, the original payment will be returned to the buyer.
